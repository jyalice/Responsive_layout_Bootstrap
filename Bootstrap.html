Sunshine and Lightning

In Alice’s room, Sunshine lives together with her elder sister Lightning. As an ordinary mouse, she has many troubles—Why multiplication tables are so difficult to recite? It’s not her but Green Grass who turns into a princess. Grandma mistakes her as the 27th granddaughter. The horror story scares her to death; even more scary is a cat named May!
